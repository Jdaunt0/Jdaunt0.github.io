What is FlyDreamAir?

FlyDreamAir is a major airline company with a large network of travel agencies and locations across the world.


How does the loyalty program work?

by signing up to our rewards program you can get points for booking accommodation with our travel agencies, booking a flight with FlyDreamAir or shopping at your local shopping center.


What can you do with these points?

there are many ways to spend your points... such as accommodation, car rentals, cheaper flights, ticket status upgrades and much more.
